status co is a cultural studio working across collectibles and collaborations.

We produce, curate and present contemporary collectibles by artists and designers through curated pop-up drops. Each drop is built around a concept, bringing together objects that speak to one another through form, idea, and context. These collectibles are created with intention; they are meant to be lived with, not stored. Positioned between art, design, and everyday life, they transform functional forms into meaningful artifacts.

Collaboration sits at the core of status co’s practice. Working closely with artists and designers, we connect the creative world with brands, institutions, and corporations. We develop collaborative projects that translate creative authorship into new contexts - whether by producing limited-edition collectibles for companies, or by matching creatives with brands to shape cultural, spatial, or strategic initiatives.

Every collaboration is conceived as a dialogue: between creator and collector, creativity and commerce, concept and use.

Across both collectibles and collaborations, status co occupies a space where authorship matters, context elevates value, and ownership becomes a form of participation.
